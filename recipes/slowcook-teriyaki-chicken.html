Slow Cooked Teriyaki Chicken

Description

I love Teriyaki chicken and I don't use my slow cooker enough, making this makes the chicken so juicy and I love the broth it makes. I highly recommend trying this one out!

Ingredients

Recipe yields 8 servings.

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Â½ cup teriyaki sauce
  • 4 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound chicken, diced

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken broth, teriyaki sauce, brown sugar, and garlic in a large bowl. Add chicken and toss with the sauce until well mixed. Pour into the slow cooker.
  2. Cook on Low until chicken is done, 4 to 6 hours.
